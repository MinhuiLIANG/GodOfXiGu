Short Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate major in computer science at BiWell Lab, City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Dr.Yuhan LUO. I am interested in using natural language processing technology and multimodal information processing technology to enhance machine intelligence, improve the way users interact and get along with machines, and make the machines become attentive and faithful assistants or partners of users. At the same time, I am very interested in the new possibilities for HCI with AIGC.
